A Quick Word

From The Owner

Opening CrossFit Lokahi has always been a dream of ours. We started off in a small, two-car garage with approximately 500 square feet, 8.5 foot ceilings, and very little equipment. It didn't stop us from working hard, learning along the way, and most importantly - building amazing relationships. We have grown from humble beginnings to a 5,400 square foot facility, 30 foot ceilings, and an abundance equipment. While our new place is capable of much more, our purpose and philosophy remain the same: work hard, learn all we can, and continue to build amazing relationships.

 

My brother and I grew up in Hawai'i and take great pride in our Hawaiian roots. Lokahi is Hawaiian for "the power of one", to be one, or a group that is of one purpose. We believe everyone can relate to this in their own way individually and collectively. In CrossFit, one more rep, one more round, one more minute is our performance goal. Hold power output and give it just one more when you feel like you can't. But it is so much more than that.

 

Lokahi is also about the power of one person. Many of our members have done amazing acts of kindness for us that it has made us better people. The power of their one act changed who we are for the better and we hope that our name will always help return the favor.

 

Another example is our decision to open CrossFit Lokahi and how it has changed so many lives. Our members have not only improved their physical health and achieved new performance goals, but have gained self-confidence, a second family, and most of all - a higher quality of life. We have countless stories to share and hope yours will be another addition.

 

We hope that your decision to join our family at Crossfit Lokahi is a decision you will reflect on as one of the best choices you have ever made in your life.
